About Us

National Institute of Technology, Calicut. One among the many dream colleges of students all over India. Ranking 23rd in the NIRF rankings and mostly ranking below 5 in all students NIT preference list. A college filled with exciting opportunities and wonderful events.

That's the good side of things. Every coin has 2 sides and so does college life here. So, here's a place to share all those stories about NITC. Be it good or bad, success or failures. Every story reveals a hidden part of this family. This is a place for you to meet like-minded individuals and make friends. A place to rant about your every-day life. A place where you can find motivation from the stories about people who lived here before you.
